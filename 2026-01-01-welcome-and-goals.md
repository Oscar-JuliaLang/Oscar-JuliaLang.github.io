## Goals for the group
- Learn OSCAR and Julia by reading docs/papers and running small experiments
- Produce concise notes that are easy to reuse
- Keep a running FAQ for common setup and conceptual questions

## Suggested session format (flexible)
1. Context (5–10 min)
2. Main topic (20–30 min)
3. Hands-on example (10–20 min)
4. Discussion + next steps (10 min)

## Proposed topics (starter list)
- Julia basics for scientific computing: environments, packages, reproducibility
- OSCAR overview: what it includes, how it fits into the ecosystem
